Delete a group chat

If you are the group admin, you can deactivate a group chat by removing all participants. This way, no one can send new messages to the group.

To remove all participants and delete a group, follow these steps:
  1. Open the group chat and tap the group subject.
  2. For each participant, tap their name, and then select Remove from Group > Remove .
  3. To remove yourself from the group, tap Exit Group.
  4. Important: After you delete the chat history from your phone, other group members can continue viewing the chat history.
    If you want to delete the chat history from your phone, tap Delete Group.
