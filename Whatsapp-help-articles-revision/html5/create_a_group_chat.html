Create a group chat

  1. Tap New Chat > New Group
  2. Select contacts to invite to the group, and then tap Next.
  3. Enter a group name.
  4. Optional: To add a group icon, tap Camera.
  5. Optional: If you prefer messages to disappear from the chat history after a while, click Disappearing Messages to select a duration. You can modify this later.
  6. Tap Create.
